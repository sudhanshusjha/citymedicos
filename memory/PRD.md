# City Medicos Website - PRD

## Original Problem Statement
Build a complete, production-ready website for a local medical store named "City Medicos – Chemist & Druggist". High-trust, call-first website for a neighborhood medical store. Primary goal: encourage customers to CALL or WHATSAPP the store.

## Core Requirements (Static)
- Phone/WhatsApp: 9873341450
- Service Highlight: FREE HOME DELIVERY
- Categories: Medicine, Ayurveda, Surgical, Cosmetics, Baby Products, Doctor Support
- Brand Color: Orange #FF4A1C, Medical Green #1BAA5E
- NO forms, NO online ordering, NO payments
- 7 pages required

## User Personas
1. **Elderly Customers**: Need medicines regularly, prefer calling over apps
2. **Busy Families**: Need home delivery for convenience
3. **New Customers**: Looking for trusted local pharmacy

## What's Been Implemented (Jan 2026)
- ✅ Complete 7-page website (Home, About, Services, Store, Contact, Privacy Policy, Terms)
- ✅ Responsive design (mobile-first)
- ✅ Sticky header with Call Now and WhatsApp CTAs
- ✅ Hero section with FREE HOME DELIVERY badge
- ✅ 6 category cards with icons
